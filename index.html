Benchmark International School (BIS), located in Tongi, Gazipur, Bangladesh, is a private educational institution established in 2022. The school offers education from Playgroup to Secondary School Certificate (SSC) level, following the National Curriculum in both Bangla and English mediums. BIS operates with the motto "Pursuit of Excellence in Education," aiming to provide a modern, activity-based learning environment that emphasizes both academic and extracurricular development.
